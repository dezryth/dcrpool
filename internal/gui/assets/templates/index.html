Mining Pool Overview

To mine, point your miner to the pool and set the username as described below in the miner configuration section.

Payments will not show up at your address as spendable until after 256 blocks at which point the coinbase matures and becomes spendable. Use the account information button at the top right to view your pending payments and other account info.

Ready to Mine? Decred is GPU Mineable!

  • If you run into any issues, please reach out to me on twitter, @dezryth. I am actively applying patches and updates as available.
  • Get and build Gominer Version 2.0.x
  • Follow directions to build from source code. Help can be found here.
  • Run gominer.exe with the following command within its directory like below:
    gominer.exe --pool stratum+tcp://{{.Address}}:{{.MinerPort}} --pooluser MININGREWARDSADDRESS.UNIQUENAMEOFYOURCHOICE

Confirm Miner Connection

To confirm a miner connection, look up its account information with its associated miner address.

The miner should be listed as a connected miner with its curent hash rate in the 'Connected Clients' section.
